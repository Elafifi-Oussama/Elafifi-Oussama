# Oussama Elafifi

Computational chemistry and molecular simulation researcher focused on molecular dynamics, cryogenic hydrogen, nanoconfinement, and high-performance computing.

## Research interests

* Molecular dynamics simulations
* Para-hydrogen and cryogenic fluids
* Hydrogen storage and transport in carbon nanotubes
* Feynman–Hibbs quantum corrections
* Lennard-Jones and effective interaction potentials
* Diffusion, radial structure, VACF, RDF, and transport properties
* HPC-based scientific computing

## Current work

I am working on molecular dynamics studies of fixed-loading para-hydrogen confined in armchair carbon nanotubes, including classical Lennard-Jones simulations and future Feynman–Hibbs quantum-corrected calculations.

Main systems:

* CNT(10,10)
* CNT(15,15)
* CNT(20,20)
* CNT(25,25)

Main analysis:

* Radial density profiles
* Axial mean-square displacement
* Self-diffusion coefficients
* Velocity autocorrelation functions
* Green–Kubo consistency checks
* Potential-energy trends
* Finite-size validation
